About Me

I am an interdisciplinary researcher exploring how artificial intelligence transforms organizations, leadership, and the future of work. My research integrates organizational behavior, public policy, communication, and management to understand the relationship between emerging technologies and human decision-making. I am currently preparing for Ph.D. studies and welcome opportunities for research collaboration.

Over the past several years, I have built interdisciplinary experience across human resources, organizational research, public policy, and AI-related projects. I also share my research, insights, and publications on topics related to artificial intelligence, leadership, and the future of work.

Spatial Analysis of Weather Station Coverage in Los Angeles

2023

Conducted a GIS-based spatial analysis of weather station distribution across Los Angeles County to evaluate geographic coverage and accessibility within different Service Planning Areas (SPAs). Using ArcGIS Pro, the project integrated spatial datasets, performed spatial joins and geoprocessing analyses, and produced thematic maps to identify spatial patterns and support evidence-based urban and environmental planning. The final output visualized weather station coverage across the county's planning regions.
